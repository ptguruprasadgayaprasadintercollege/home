Welcome to PGGIC

Pt. Guruprasad Gayaprasad Inter College

It is a private intermediate institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Located in Nauranga, Ghatmapur, Kanpur Nagar (Uttar Pradesh). Our college has consistently ranked among the top-performing schools in the UP Board examinations year after year.

The Mission of the School is to provide the right inputs to help the children grow up into caring and sharing individuals, equipped to make the right choices in life and grow up to be responsible citizens of the country and the world.

"Education is not just about books and exams; it is about shaping character, nurturing curiosity, and inspiring lifelong learning. A true school empowers students to dream, to act with integrity, and to contribute meaningfully to society."

~ Mr. Ved Prakash Dwivedi ( Principal )
